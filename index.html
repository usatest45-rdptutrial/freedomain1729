Interesting Facts About Web Hosting

1. The first website, created by Tim Berners-Lee in 1991, was hosted on a NeXT computer at CERN.

2. The first domain name ever registered was symbolics.com in 1985.

3. In the early days of the internet, hosting was expensive, and only large corporations and universities could afford it.

4. Today, cloud hosting has revolutionized the industry, making websites faster and more secure.
